QHSE Mission

Our QHSE mission is to ensure that quality, health, safety, and environmental considerations remain top priorities for management and all employees. Prevention of accidental risk and loss from process failure becomes a recognized, integral part of our continuous improvement culture.

QHSE Culture

QHSE, a line management responsibility, requires visible commitment, leadership, and involvement. All employees shall understand, share, and practice our proactive QHSE culture as an integral part of everyday business. Fundamental to this culture is our belief that all losses of people, property, and processes result from management failure and are preventable.

QHSE Strategy

No incidents due to non-compliance.

Zero defect culture shall be attained through:

  • Visible commitment, leadership, and involvement of line management tied to incentive objectives standards of mandatory.
  • QHSE training for all personnel based on respective roles.
  • Maximized efficiency and synergy through the sharing of resources.

QHSE integration in all product development Proactive participation in global industry initiatives and recognition by our customers that we attain and stand with our international competitors.

National Petroleum Services Company (NAPESCO) was founded in 1993 as a private company offering pumping services to the Kuwait Energy Sector. NAPESCO has established itself as a leading provider of Upstream Oilfield Services including Cementing, Stimulation, Coiled Tubing, Nitrogen, and Downhole Service Tools.
