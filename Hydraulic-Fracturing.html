Hydraulic Fracturing

Hydraulic fracturing, also known as fracking, involves injecting high-pressure fluid into subterranean rock formations to create fractures, facilitating the flow of oil or gas.

Our hydraulic fracturing services use state-of-the-art equipment and environmentally friendly fluids to maximize hydrocarbon recovery while minimizing ecological impact.

With a focus on safety and efficiency, we tailor our fracking operations to the specific geological conditions of each well, ensuring optimal fracture propagation and reservoir stimulation.

Advanced monitoring technologies and data analysis allow for precise control and assessment of the fracturing process, enhancing overall productivity.

National Petroleum Services Company (NAPESCO) was founded in 1993 as a private company offering pumping services to the Kuwait Energy Sector. NAPESCO has established itself as a leading provider of Upstream Oilfield Services including Cementing, Stimulation, Coiled Tubing, Nitrogen, and Downhole Service Tools.
