Environmental Consultancy

As a leader in environmental consulting, NAPESCO holds the distinction of being the only company in Kuwait licensed by the Environmental Public Authority (EPA) for all four categories of environmental services. Our services, recognized and trusted by all state-owned Oil & Gas companies, include:

  • Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (ESIA) Studies.
  • Evaluating Existing Environmental Conditions.
  • Environmental Auditing and Consultations.
  • Advanced Modeling for Air Dispersion, Noise, Hydrodynamics, Groundwater, and Oil Spills

Our team, composed of world-class scientists and engineers, leverages extensive industry knowledge and state-of-the-art software to execute over 300 successful ESIA studies for major stakeholders like KOC, KNPC, WJO, and MEWRE.

Environmental Monitoring & Testing Laboratory

NAPESCO’s ISO-certified laboratory is equipped to conduct rigorous environmental tests ensuring health and safety standards are met. Our capabilities include:

  • Air Quality, Stack Emissions, Vehicles Emissions Monitoring.
  • Water, Wastewater, and Soil Analysis.
  • Noise, Light, and Vibration Monitoring.
  • Advanced Oil and Gas Sampling and Analysis.
  • Microbiological and Chemical Testing.

These services are critical in assessing impacts, ensuring regulatory compliance, and facilitating research and development within the environmental domain.

Geo-technical Services

Our geo-technical division offers tailored solutions to complex challenges. With over two decades of expertise, our certified professionals provide:

    Field Reconnaissance and Desk Top Studies.
  • Comprehensive Field Explorations and Investigations.
  • In-Situ and Laboratory Testing.
  • Detailed Engineering Analysis and Geotechnical Reporting.

Industrial Waste Management Services

Pollution defined as the 'Undesirable Elements in the Environment', affects life and property adversely. Pollution occurs naturally or through manmade sources and exists in the form of Air, Water, Soil, Noise, Hazardous and Radioactive Pollutants. A proper Environment Management Program including Waste Minimization and Management is required as these Pollutants have long lasting effects in the environment.

Waste Management Services offered by include:

  • Characterization & Quantification of Solid Waste (Industrial & Hazardous).
  • Decontamination.
  • Transportation & Disposal.
  • Recovery, Recycling & Utilization.
  • Landfill Remediation & Rehabilitation
  • Long Term Solid Waste Management Plans.
  • Industrial Wastewater Treatment.

NAPESCO specializes in providing treatment and remediation of hazardous waste occurring from oil sludge pits, effluent pits, drill cuttings & contaminated soil using various techniques including:

  • Thermal Desorption.
  • Electrocoagulation.
  • Oil Recovery & Sludge Treatment.
  • Soil Washing.
  • Bio Remediation.
  • Phyto Remediation.
  • Land Reclamation and Rehabilitation.

Our Commitment

At NAPESCO, we are committed to delivering not just solutions, but also value to our clients by understanding their needs and surpassing their expectations. Our goal is to foster a sustainable future through strategic environmental stewardship, technological innovation, and uncompromised quality in our services.

National Petroleum Services Company (NAPESCO) was founded in 1993 as a private company offering pumping services to the Kuwait Energy Sector. NAPESCO has established itself as a leading provider of Upstream Oilfield Services including Cementing, Stimulation, Coiled Tubing, Nitrogen, and Downhole Service Tools.
